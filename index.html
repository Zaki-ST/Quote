Website Development & Hosting Proposal

Project: Institutional Website (AMER)
Model: Agile Development
Tech: WordPress

Executive Summary

This proposal provides a comprehensive quote for the development and hosting of a new institutional website for AMER. We propose a user-friendly WordPress platform, featuring institutional pages, News/Blog, Events, a Members directory, and Contact forms. The site will be fully bilingual (Portuguese/English), responsive, and SEO-optimized.
