Website Development & Hosting Proposal

Project: Institutional Website – Renewable Energy Sector (AMER)
Delivery Model: Agile Development Methodology
Technology: WordPress (Premium Vendor Template)

Executive Summary

This proposal provides a comprehensive quote for the development and hosting of a new institutional website for AMER, an association in the renewable energy sector. We propose a user-friendly WordPress platform, featuring institutional pages, News/Blog, Events, a Members/Partners directory, and Contact forms. The site will be fully bilingual (Portuguese/English), responsive, and SEO-optimized, with its design taking inspiration from the ALER website. This document details the development quote, recurring hosting and maintenance costs and estimated timeline.

0. Pricing Methodology

The cost estimates provided in this proposal are calculated based on an estimated professional hourly rate. This rate covers all aspects of service delivery, including analysis, project coordination, design, development, quality assurance, security, and deployment.

For transparent calculation, an estimated blended professional hourly rate of 3,000 MZN per hour has been applied to base development components, and 4,000 MZN per hour for optional modules and specialized services.

All development and recurring costs are presented with clear ranges. Fixed prices can be established upon detailed scope finalization, ensuring budget predictability for the agreed services.

1. Project Approach

The website will be developed using a reliable, premium WordPress theme. This approach ensures long-term maintainability and allows for cost-efficient delivery without compromising quality. The solution will be specifically tailored to the operational and communication needs of a renewable energy association working in an international context. The visual design will draw inspiration from leading institutional sites in the sector, such as the ALER (www.aler-energia.org) website, to achieve a modern, professional, and intuitive user experience.

  • Cost-efficient delivery without compromising quality
  • Scalable and easy-to-maintain architecture
  • Clear separation between base scope and optional enhancements
  • Design inspiration from leading sector websites like ALER

2. Scope of Work

  • WordPress CMS for easy content management and updates
  • Institutional pages (e.g., About Us, Mission, Vision, Governance, Members)
  • Dynamic News / Blog section for regular updates and announcements
  • Dedicated Events section for upcoming and past activities, with filters
  • Comprehensive Members / Partners directory, potentially with search functionality
  • User-friendly Contact forms with spam protection
  • Fully bilingual structure (Portuguese / English) for broad international reach
  • SEO-friendly architecture for improved search engine visibility
  • Responsive design ensuring optimal viewing and functionality on all devices (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Basic accessibility considerations (WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines where applicable)
