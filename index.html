Website Development & Hosting Proposal

Client: AMER (Renewable Energy)
Technology: WordPress (Premium Architecture)

01. Executive Summary

This proposal details the development and hosting of a new institutional website for AMER. We propose a modern, bilingual (Portuguese/English) WordPress platform featuring Members Directories, Events, News, and secure contact forms. The site will be fully responsive and optimized for SEO.

02. Pricing Methodology
